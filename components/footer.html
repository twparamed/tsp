Taiwan Society of Paramedicine

台灣醫療救護學會 (TSP) 是一個專業的非營利組織，致力於到院前救護的研究、教育與政策推廣，與世界急救標準接軌。 TSP is a professional non-profit organization dedicated to research, education, and policy advocacy in pre-hospital care, aligning with global EMS standards.
