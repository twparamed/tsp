迎向挑戰，引領台灣 EMS 邁向卓越

各位緊急醫療救護的同仁、學會會員與各界先進，大家好：

緊急醫療救護（EMS）是守護民眾生命安全的第一道防線，也是衡量一個國家醫療進步程度的重要指標。近年來，隨著高齡化社會的來臨以及各種新興災害的挑戰，第一線救護人員所肩負的責任日益重大，我們所需的專業知能與臨床決策能力，也面臨著前所未有的考驗。

「台灣醫療救護學會 (TSP)」成立的初衷，正是為了凝聚全國 EMS 菁英的力量，將台灣的到院前救護推向更高的專業層次。我們深信，唯有透過扎實的學術研究、與國際接軌的教育訓練，以及積極參與政策倡議，才能真正提升整體救護品質，並為救護技術員建立更完善的執業環境。

展望未來，學會將持續推動以下三大核心目標：

  • 深化實證醫學基礎： 持續推動「台灣醫療救護學會期刊」，鼓勵臨床研究發表，讓每一項急救處置都有堅實的科學證據支持。
  • 擴展國際專業交流： 透過 PERIC 等國際研討會與專案計畫，引進世界頂尖的急重症救護指引，讓台灣的 EMS 實力在國際舞台上發光。
  • 建構完善教育體系： 推動各項進階持續教育 (CPD) 與專業認證，協助所有救護同仁在職涯中不斷精進。

每一位在街頭奮戰的救護人員，都是拯救生命的英雄。學會將永遠做各位最堅強的後盾。讓我們攜手並進，共同為台灣的緊急醫療救護寫下嶄新的一頁，為這片土地上的人民提供更安全、更專業的醫療保障。

范傑閔 台灣醫療救護學會 理事長

Embracing Challenges, Leading Taiwan's EMS to Excellence

Dear EMS colleagues, Society members, and distinguished guests,

Emergency Medical Services (EMS) is the first line of defense in safeguarding public lives and a crucial indicator of a nation's medical advancement. In recent years, with the advent of an aging society and the challenges of emerging disasters, the responsibilities of frontline paramedics have grown immensely. The professional knowledge and clinical decision-making skills we require are facing unprecedented tests.

The founding aspiration of the "Taiwan Society of Paramedicine (TSP)" is to unite the strength of EMS elites nationwide to elevate Taiwan's pre-hospital care to a higher level of professionalism. We firmly believe that only through solid academic research, internationally aligned education and training, and active policy advocacy can we truly improve the overall quality of care and build a better practice environment for paramedics.

Looking ahead, the Society will continue to drive three core objectives:

  • Deepening the Foundation of Evidence-Based Medicine: We will continue to promote the TSP Journal, encouraging the publication of clinical research so that every emergency intervention is backed by solid scientific evidence.
  • Expanding International Professional Exchange: Through international symposiums and projects like PERIC, we aim to introduce the world's leading critical care guidelines, allowing Taiwan's EMS capabilities to shine on the global stage.
  • Building a Comprehensive Education System: We will promote advanced Continuing Professional Development (CPD) and certifications, assisting all EMS professionals in their continuous career advancement.

Every paramedic fighting on the streets is a hero saving lives. The Society will always be your strongest backer. Let us work hand in hand to write a new chapter for Taiwan's emergency medical services and provide safer, more professional medical protection for the people of this land.

Jie-Min Fan, MD President, Taiwan Society of Paramedicine
