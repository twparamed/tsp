委員會介紹

Committees

台灣醫療救護學會透過三大核心委員會的專業分工，致力於推動到院前救護的實證研究、精進教育訓練品質，並全方位守護第一線救護人員的職業安全與身心健康。

TSP is dedicated to advancing pre-hospital evidence-based research, refining training quality, and comprehensively safeguarding the occupational health and safety of frontline paramedics through our three core committees.

學術與出版委員會

Academic & Publication

致力於建立台灣緊急醫療救護的實證醫學基礎，推動國內外救護學術交流，並將最新的臨床研究成果轉化為實際的救護指引。

Dedicated to building the foundation of evidence-based medicine for Taiwan's EMS, promoting international academic exchange, and translating clinical research into practical guidelines.

  • 學術期刊發行與徵稿 TSP Journal Publication
  • 策辦學術研討會與活動 Academic Conferences & Events
  • PERIC 專案推動與數據分析 PERIC Project & Data Analysis

教育委員會

Education Committee

核心目標在於提升各級救護人員的專業知能與臨床決策能力，引進國際標準化課程，並培育優秀的本土臨床指導員。

Focuses on enhancing the professional skills and clinical decision-making of paramedics, introducing international standardized courses, and cultivating excellent local clinical preceptors.

  • 高階救護員繼續教育 (CME) Paramedic Continuing Medical Education
  • 到院前創傷生命救援 (PHTLS) PHTLS Certification Courses
  • 戰術/戰傷救護 (TCCC/TECC) TCCC / TECC Training
  • 臨床指導員訓練機制建立 Clinical Preceptor Training

職業安全與衛生委員會

OH&S Committee

唯有健康的救護員，才能提供高品質的救護。本委員會致力於政策倡議與實務計畫，全方位守護同仁的生理與心理健康。

Healthy paramedics are the foundation of high-quality care. This committee focuses on policy advocacy and practical programs to protect both the physical and mental well-being of our peers.

  • 友善執業環境與政策倡議 Policy Advocacy for Safe Practice
  • 推動「保背計畫」
    優化 EMT 安全徒手搬負 (Manual Handling)     Save-the-Back Project
    Safe Manual Handling for EMTs
  • 心理健康與同儕支持 (Peer Support)
    關注創傷後壓力與復原力     Mental Health & Peer Support
    Addressing PTSD and Resilience
  • 學會亮點專案
    EMS職業安全衛生平台
