Training Courses

提供與國際接軌的專業緊急醫療救護課程，全面提升第一線人員的臨床決策與急救技術。

核心訓練模組 Core Training Modules

高級救護員繼續教育 (CME)

Paramedic CME

專為 EMT-P 設計的進階持續教育課程，涵蓋最新實證醫學指引、特殊急重症處置與困難案例研討，滿足展延所需學分。

Advanced continuing education designed for EMT-P, covering the latest evidence-based guidelines, critical care management, and complex case studies.

到院前創傷生命救援 (PHTLS)

PHTLS Course

引進 NAEMT 國際標準課程，強調創傷機轉評估與黃金時間內的關鍵處置，提升重大創傷病患的存活率。

NAEMT standard course focusing on trauma kinematics and critical interventions within the golden hour to improve survival rates.

戰術與戰傷救護 (TCCC/TECC)

TCCC / TECC

針對高風險與敵意環境下的急救訓練，包含大量傷患處置、止血帶應用與安全撤離策略。

Training for high-risk and hostile environments, including mass casualty management, tourniquet application, and safe evacuation.

臨床指導員訓練 (Preceptor)

Clinical Preceptor Training

培育優秀的救護教學人才，學習成人教育理論、臨床帶教技巧與客觀評估方法，傳承救護專業。

Cultivating EMS educators by teaching adult learning theories, clinical mentoring skills, and objective evaluation methods.
