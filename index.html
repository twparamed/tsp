About TSP

推動台灣 EMS 專業化與國際化的推手 Advancing EMS Professionalism Globally

台灣醫療救護學會成立的宗旨在於促進緊急醫療救護之研究與發展。我們透過建立學術平台、推廣高品質的教育訓練，以及積極參與政策倡議，致力於提升第一線救護人員的臨床決策能力與執業環境。 Our mission is to promote the research and development of emergency medical services. Through academic platforms, high-quality education, and policy advocacy, we strive to elevate clinical decision-making capabilities and practice environments for frontline paramedics.
